Army Strategy: Idle Game

Thank you for considering my mobile game. My game enhances user functionality by providing an immersive and entertaining gaming experience that can be enjoyed on-the-go.

About the Game

My game is a strategy game that challenges players to create strategies to defeat enemies, and gain currency. With challenging and in-depth gameplay, my game is designed to provide hours of fun and excitement.

Features

  • In-depth army creation and customization
  • Hundreds of different things to research and buy
  • Compete with friends on the leaderboards and earn achievements
  • Cloud saving so that you can play the same save on multiple devices

Requirements

To play my game, users must have a compatible mobile device running Android 4.4 or later. Also to receive full functioality of cloud saving users must be signed in with Google Play Games and have a connection to the internet.

Privacy Policy

My game respects your privacy and does not collect any personal information that is not needed for the functionality of the game. My game also uses third-party analytics tools to collect non-personal data such as device type, screen resolution, and gameplay statistics. For more information, please read the privacy policy.
